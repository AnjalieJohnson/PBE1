About Us


Inspired by Thailand, French, East Europian and English cuisines. Which is reflected in the menu and ambiance. There is an extensive menu for coffee lovers alongside teas and smoothies. Our primary focus and driving philosophy behind Cafe Hoku food is introducing diners to well curated Thailand style dishes.
